ABOUT US

Want to be updated on all the info about tech? In this rapidly changing world, it is indeed mandatory for students to keep themselves updated about technology. Worry not, We here, Q-Hub are an interactive zone where we conduct quizzes in order to keep aware of the latest Technical stuff, Science, Technology and share it with the entire community.

Students prepare, participate and host quizzes on various topics. The objective of our club is to foster a desire for knowledge, to expand the intellectual, mental, and creative ability of the students while infusing a sense of healthy competition among them.

Code Fiesta

About the Event

Q-Hub mastermind it's second event on 19-12-2021, which was a coding quiz and was held on D2C, an online platform. As it was inter college event students from different collages had participated in the event and made it a great success.

This event repressed 2 rounds where round-1 contains 20 MCQ questions on basic programming language which was held for 25 min. People who were qualified in round-1 has taken Round-2 which contains 2 sections where section-1 has 4 questions and a time limit of 10min and Section has 4 approach based questions of time limit 10min. A participation certificate was given to all the participants, the people who were qualified for the second round were appreciated with a merit certificate and the people who won in round-2 were awarded with a winning certificate.

Event Concluded

Quizeria

About the Event

A Quiz event organised by QHUB VIT. This event will be conducted in 2 parts.

1. Technical Part (2:30PM to 4:00PM)
Round-1: A quiz will be conducted based on current affairs to check the general knowledge of participants.
Round-2: Another quiz will be conducted based on the topic “Block Chain” as this might be the booming topic in the upcoming future.
Round-3: “Marketing”, a sample product will be given to the participants, for which they need to explain briefly that how they are going to bring the product in to the market as a business man.

2. Non-Technical Part (4:00PM to 5:30PM)
This Non-Technical part of the event consists of some cultural activities like dance and singing. And finally an open stage will be given to all the participants to participate in the cultural part as well.

Eligibility: All 21, 20, 19, 18 batch students are invited to take part in.
